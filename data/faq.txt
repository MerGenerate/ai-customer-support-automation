Customer Support FAQ

1. Where is my order?
You can track your order using the tracking link sent to your email after purchase. If you cannot find the email, please contact support with your order number.

2. How can I return a product?
You can request a return within 14 days of delivery if the product meets the return conditions. The product must be unused and in its original packaging.

3. How long does delivery take?
Standard delivery usually takes 3 to 7 business days depending on your location. International delivery may take longer.

4. Do you offer refunds?
Yes, refunds are available for eligible returns after inspection. Refunds are typically processed within 5 to 10 business days.

5. How can I contact support?
You can contact support via email, phone, or live chat during business hours. Please include your order number for faster assistance.

6. What should I do if my item is damaged?
If you receive a damaged item, contact support within 48 hours. Include photos of the product and packaging.

7. Can I change my order after placing it?
Order changes are possible within a limited time after purchase. Please contact support immediately to request changes.

8. Do you offer international shipping?
Yes, we ship internationally. Shipping times and costs vary depending on the destination.
